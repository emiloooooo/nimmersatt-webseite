4. How the site works

The site is a scroll-based portfolio. Image sequences and text for each project load from this site's own static assets. The navigation in the top-right corner opens a menu that lists all projects; clicking a project — or clicking anywhere on the scene — opens a built-in video player modal directly on the page.

The player is our own implementation; no third-party player is embedded. No YouTube, Vimeo, or similar service is loaded, and no tracking pixels, analytics scripts or advertising networks are used. There is no comment function, no share widget, and no social-media login.

5. Videos & media delivery

Project videos and the preloader animation are delivered as static files from GitHub Pages. When you play a clip, your browser requests that file from GitHub, which — as with any web request — means GitHub temporarily sees your IP address and user-agent. No cookies are set in the process, and we do not receive or store this data ourselves.

6. Cookies & local storage

This website does not set any cookies of its own. No tracking or advertising cookies are used. A short sound effect that plays when you hover over menu entries runs entirely in the browser and does not transmit any data.
